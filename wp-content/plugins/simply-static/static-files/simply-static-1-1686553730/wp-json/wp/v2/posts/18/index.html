Novak Djokovic claimed a record-breaking 23rd men’s singles Grand Slam title when he beat Norway’s Casper Ruud 7-6(1) 6-3 7-5 in the French Open final on Sunday.<\/p>\n

The Serbian is now the only male player to have won each of the four Grand Slams – Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon and US Open – at least three times.<\/p>\n

Fourth seed Ruud, playing in his third major final after Roland Garros and the US Open, got off to the better start with an early break.<\/p>\n

Djokovic, however, rallied back to win the tiebreak, then cruised through the second set and tightened his grip when it mattered in the third.<\/p>\n
